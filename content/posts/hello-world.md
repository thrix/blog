Hello! I'm Miroslav Vadkerti, known as **thrix** online. I'm a Sr Principal
Software Engineer at [Red Hat](https://www.redhat.com/), where I lead the
[Testing Farm](https://testing-farm.io/) team. Our work focuses on
building CI testing infrastructure for
[RHEL](https://www.redhat.com/en/technologies/linux-platforms/enterprise-linux),
[Fedora](https://fedoraproject.org/), and
[CentOS Stream](https://www.centos.org/centos-stream/) -- making it easier for
developers and maintainers to test their packages and changes across these
distributions.

Some of the upstream projects I contribute to:

- [tmt](https://github.com/teemtee/tmt) -- Test Management Tool, a framework
  for defining and running tests
- [Testing Farm CLI](https://gitlab.com/testing-farm/cli) -- command-line
  interface for the Testing Farm service
- [Artemis](https://gitlab.com/testing-farm/artemis) -- provisioning backend
  for testing infrastructure

Outside of work, I'm a Linux desktop enthusiast running
[Fedora Silverblue](https://fedoraproject.org/atomic-desktops/silverblue/) with
a custom [BlueBuild](https://blue-build.org/) image, and I occasionally tinker
with [Nix](https://nixos.org/). I also maintain
[nix-toolbox](https://thrix.github.io/nix-toolbox/) -- a Fedora toolbox
integration with Nix.

## Why a blog?

I often run into problems that take time to solve. Once I find a solution, it
lives in my head or in scattered notes. A blog is a better home for these
solutions -- both for my future self and for anyone else who runs into the same
issues.

## What to expect

Posts here will mostly be about:

- **Linux desktop** -- Fedora, Silverblue/BlueBuild, and the desktop Linux
  experience
- **Testing and CI** -- testing infrastructure, automation, and developer
  tooling
- **Open source** -- tools, workflows, and contributions
- **Technology** -- anything interesting I stumble across
